5. Scanner and Camera
CARDORA requests access to your device's camera only when you choose to scan a trading card.
Camera access is used exclusively for card recognition and is never activated without your permission.
Images captured during scanning are processed only for the purpose of identifying trading cards and retrieving related card information.
CARDORA does not permanently store photos captured through the scanner unless you explicitly choose to save content that requires image storage in a future feature.
After a successful recognition, CARDORA uses the official trading card image provided by the external trading card database instead of storing the photo taken with your device whenever possible.
You may deny camera permission at any time through your device settings. If camera access is denied, camera-based scanning will not be available, but other supported features of the application may continue to function.
6. Photo Library Access
CARDORA may request permission to access your device's photo library when you choose to recognize a trading card from an existing image.
Access to your photo library is always initiated by you and is used only for the selected image.
CARDORA does not scan your entire photo library, does not continuously monitor your photos, and does not access images that you have not explicitly selected.
Selected images are processed only for trading card recognition. Unless a future feature explicitly requires image storage and you choose to use it, CARDORA does not permanently retain these images after processing.
You may revoke photo library permission at any time through your device settings. Revoking this permission will prevent image-based card recognition while leaving other application features available.
7. Pokémon TCG API
CARDORA uses the Pokémon Trading Card Game API ("Pokémon TCG API") to retrieve official trading card information, including card names, images, set information, rarity, market data, and other publicly available metadata.
When a card is recognized through the scanner or searched within the application, CARDORA may send limited search information necessary to identify the requested card.
CARDORA does not transmit your password, payment information, or personal collection data to the Pokémon TCG API.
The Pokémon TCG API is operated by an independent third party and is subject to its own privacy practices and terms of use. CARDORA is not responsible for the privacy policies or data handling practices of third-party services.
If the Pokémon TCG API is temporarily unavailable, certain card lookup features may not function until the service becomes available again.
