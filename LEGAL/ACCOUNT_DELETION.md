# CARDORA Account Deletion

## Overview

CARDORA allows users to permanently delete their account and associated personal data.

Users may request account deletion directly within the application or by contacting CARDORA Support.

---

## Delete Your Account in the App

1. Sign in to your CARDORA account.
2. Open **Profile**.
3. Open **Settings**.
4. Select **Delete Account**.
5. Read the confirmation message.
6. Confirm the deletion request.

Your account will then be permanently deleted.

---

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, CARDORA permanently removes:

* User account
* Profile information
* Collection
* Wishlist
* Favorites
* Portfolio data
* Personal preferences
* Founder status (if applicable)
* Early Supporter status (if applicable)
* Subscription information stored by CARDORA

---

## What Data May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained when required by law or for fraud prevention purposes.

Google Play purchase records and payment information are managed by Google and are not stored by CARDORA.

---

## Deletion Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed as soon as reasonably possible.

Most requests are completed within a few minutes, although some systems may require additional synchronization time.
