
# CARDORA Membership

**Status:** Active

---

# Overview

CARDORA offers multiple membership types designed to support both casual collectors and dedicated community members.

Each membership provides different benefits while maintaining a fair experience for all users.

---

# Membership Types

CARDORA currently supports:

* Free
* Founder
* Early Supporter
* Pro

---

# Free Membership

The Free membership is available to every registered user.

Features include:

* Trading card collection
* Card scanner
* Vault
* Wishlist
* Favorites
* Portfolio
* Profile

Advertisements may be displayed.

---

# Founder Membership

Founder Membership is a permanent one-time purchase.

Benefits include:

* Permanent Founder Badge
* Unique Founder Number
* Founder Certificate
* Founder Recognition
* Ad-Free Experience
* Future Founder rewards (if introduced)

Founder memberships are limited in quantity.

---

# Early Supporter Membership

Early Supporter Membership is a permanent one-time purchase.

Benefits include:

* Permanent Early Supporter Badge
* Unique Early Supporter Number
* Community Recognition

Availability is limited.

---

# Pro Membership

Pro Membership is a recurring subscription.

Benefits include:

* Ad-Free Experience
* Premium features
* Future Pro-exclusive functionality
* Priority access to selected features

The available features may expand in future releases.

---

# Membership Verification

Membership status is securely synchronized with the backend.

Verification includes:

* Purchase validation
* Subscription status
* Membership activation
* Membership expiration (Pro)

---

# Purchase Providers

Android purchases are processed through:

* Google Play Billing

Future iOS purchases will be processed through:

* Apple In-App Purchases

---

# Membership Security

Membership information is protected through:

* Secure authentication
* Backend verification
* Purchase validation
* Row Level Security (RLS)

---

# Future Expansion

Future membership enhancements may include:

* Additional Pro benefits
* Community rewards
* Loyalty programs
* Exclusive Founder events
