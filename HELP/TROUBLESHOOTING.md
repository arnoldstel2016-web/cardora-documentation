# CARDORA Troubleshooting

**Status:** Active

---

# Overview

This guide provides solutions for common issues that users may encounter while using CARDORA.

Before contacting support, users are encouraged to review the solutions below.

---

# Authentication

## Unable to Sign In

Possible causes:

* Incorrect email or password
* Internet connection unavailable
* Authentication service temporarily unavailable

Possible solutions:

* Verify your email and password.
* Reset your password if necessary.
* Check your internet connection.
* Try signing in again later.

---

# Scanner

## Camera Does Not Open

Possible causes:

* Camera permission denied
* Camera in use by another application
* Device limitation

Possible solutions:

* Grant camera permission.
* Close other camera applications.
* Restart CARDORA.

---

## Card Not Recognized

Possible causes:

* Poor lighting
* Blurry image
* Card partially hidden
* Unsupported card

Possible solutions:

* Improve lighting.
* Hold the device steady.
* Capture the full card.
* Try scanning again.

---

## Gallery Import Failed

Possible causes:

* Unsupported image format
* Corrupted image
* Storage permission denied

Possible solutions:

* Choose another image.
* Verify photo permissions.
* Restart the application.

---

# Collection

## Card Not Saved

Possible causes:

* Network issue
* Temporary server problem

Possible solutions:

* Check your internet connection.
* Try saving the card again.
* Wait a few moments and retry.

---

## Collection Not Synchronizing

Possible causes:

* Internet connection unavailable
* Synchronization delay

Possible solutions:

* Refresh your collection.
* Sign out and sign back in.
* Wait for synchronization to complete.

---

# Purchases

## Purchase Failed

Possible causes:

* Payment declined
* Google Play Billing error
* Apple payment issue

Possible solutions:

* Verify your payment method.
* Retry the purchase.
* Contact your store provider if necessary.

---

## Subscription Not Active

Possible causes:

* Purchase verification still processing
* Synchronization delay

Possible solutions:

* Use Restore Purchases.
* Restart the application.
* Wait a few minutes.

---

# Performance

## Application Is Slow

Possible causes:

* Poor internet connection
* Low device memory

Possible solutions:

* Restart the application.
* Close background applications.
* Restart your device.

---

## Images Not Loading

Possible causes:

* Network issue
* API temporarily unavailable

Possible solutions:

* Refresh the page.
* Verify your internet connection.
* Try again later.

---

# Still Need Help?

If your issue cannot be resolved using this guide, please contact CARDORA Support.
