# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Status:** Active

---

# General

## What is CARDORA?

CARDORA is a cross-platform trading card collection application that helps collectors scan, organize, manage, and track their collections.

---

## Which trading card games are currently supported?

Currently supported:

* Pokémon Trading Card Game

Planned future support:

* Magic: The Gathering
* One Piece Card Game
* Yu-Gi-Oh!

---

## Is CARDORA free?

Yes.

CARDORA offers a free version with core collection features.

Additional memberships, including Founder, Early Supporter, and Pro, provide extra benefits.

---

## Which platforms are supported?

Currently:

* Android
* Web

Planned:

* iOS

---

# Scanner

## How do I scan a card?

Open the Scanner, point your camera at a trading card, and allow CARDORA to identify it automatically.

---

## Can I scan cards from my photo library?

Yes.

CARDORA supports both camera scanning and image import from your photo library.

---

## Why wasn't my card recognized?

Recognition accuracy may be affected by:

* Poor lighting
* Blurry images
* Covered card text
* Unsupported cards
* Network connectivity

Try scanning again with better lighting and focus.

---

# Collection

## Can I edit my collection?

Yes.

You can manage your Vault, Favorites, Wishlist, and Portfolio at any time.

---

## Does my collection sync between devices?

Yes.

Your collection is securely synchronized with your CARDORA account.

---

# Membership

## What is Founder Membership?

Founder Membership is a limited one-time purchase that permanently recognizes early supporters of CARDORA.

---

## What is Early Supporter?

Early Supporter is a limited membership available during the initial release period.

---

## What is Pro Membership?

Pro is a monthly subscription that provides premium features and an ad-free experience.

---

# Purchases

## How do I restore purchases?

Use the Restore Purchases option within the application.

Google Play or Apple will verify eligible purchases.

---

## Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes.

Subscriptions can be managed through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

---

# Privacy

## Does CARDORA store my photos?

No.

Images are processed only for card recognition and are not stored for advertising purposes.

---

## Can I delete my account?

Yes.

Account deletion is available through the application.

Additional information is available in the Account Deletion documentation.
