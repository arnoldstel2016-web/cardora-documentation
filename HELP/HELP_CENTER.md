# Getting Started

## Create an Account

1. Open CARDORA.
2. Select **Create Account**.
3. Enter your email address and password.
4. Verify your email if required.
5. Sign in to your new account.

---

## Sign In

CARDORA supports:

* Email and Password
* Google Sign-In
* Apple Sign-In (when available)

---

# Scanner

## Scan Using Camera

1. Open the Scanner.
2. Allow camera permission.
3. Point the camera at a trading card.
4. Wait for recognition.
5. Confirm the detected card.
6. Save the card to your Vault.

---

## Scan From Photo Library

1. Open the Scanner.
2. Select **Photo Library**.
3. Choose an image.
4. Wait for recognition.
5. Confirm the result.
6. Save the card.
