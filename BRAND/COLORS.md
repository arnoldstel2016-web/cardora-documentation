# CARDORA Color Palette

**Status:** Active

---

# Overview

The CARDORA color palette defines the official colors used throughout the application, website, documentation, certificates, and marketing materials.

Maintaining a consistent color palette strengthens the CARDORA brand identity and ensures a professional user experience.

---

# Primary Colors

## CARDORA Black

Purpose:

Primary backgrounds and premium sections.

Suggested Color:

#0F0F10

---

## CARDORA Gold

Purpose:

Founder features, premium highlights, certificates, and important UI elements.

Suggested Color:

#D4AF37

---

## CARDORA White

Purpose:

Primary text on dark backgrounds and clean interface elements.

Suggested Color:

#FFFFFF

---

# Secondary Colors

## Success Green

Purpose:

Successful actions, confirmations, active memberships.

Suggested Color:

#22C55E

---

## Warning Orange

Purpose:

Warnings and important notifications.

Suggested Color:

#F59E0B

---

## Error Red

Purpose:

Errors, failed operations, destructive actions.

Suggested Color:

#EF4444

---

## Information Blue

Purpose:

Information messages and neutral notifications.

Suggested Color:

#3B82F6

---

# Neutral Colors

Recommended grayscale palette for:

* Backgrounds
* Borders
* Cards
* Disabled elements
* Secondary text

---

# Accessibility

Colors should always provide sufficient contrast to ensure readability and accessibility.

Avoid relying solely on color to communicate important information.

---

# Brand Consistency

All applications, documentation, websites, and marketing materials should use the official CARDORA color palette whenever possible.
