# CARDORA Logo Usage

**Status:** Active

---

# Overview

The CARDORA logo is the primary visual identifier of the brand.

This document defines how the official logo should be used across applications, websites, documentation, marketing materials, and social media.

---

# Official Logo

The official CARDORA logo must always be used in its original form.

Do not modify, recreate, or distort the logo.

---

# Correct Usage

The logo may be used on:

* Mobile applications
* Official website
* Documentation
* Marketing materials
* Social media
* Presentations
* Certificates

---

# Clear Space

Always leave sufficient empty space around the logo.

No text, icons, or graphics should interfere with the logo's visibility.

---

# Minimum Size

The logo should remain clearly readable at all sizes.

Avoid using the logo below the minimum readable size.

---

# Incorrect Usage

The logo must never:

* Be stretched
* Be compressed
* Be rotated
* Be recolored without approval
* Have visual effects applied
* Be placed on unreadable backgrounds
* Be modified or partially hidden

---

# Backgrounds

Preferred backgrounds include:

* White
* Black
* Official CARDORA colors

Ensure sufficient contrast for readability.

---

# File Formats

Official logo files may include:

* SVG
* PNG
* JPG (where appropriate)

SVG should be preferred whenever possible.

---

# Ownership

The CARDORA logo is the intellectual property of CARDORA.

Unauthorized modification or commercial use is prohibited without permission.
