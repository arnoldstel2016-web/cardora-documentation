# CARDORA Founder Certificate

**Status:** Active

---

# Overview

The Founder Certificate is an official digital certificate issued by CARDORA to every verified Founder Member.

It serves as permanent recognition for supporting CARDORA during its early development.

Each certificate is unique and permanently associated with the Founder account.

---

# Purpose

The Founder Certificate recognizes users who joined the Founder Program and helped support the initial release of CARDORA.

The certificate is intended to be a collectible digital achievement.

---

# Certificate Information

Each Founder Certificate includes:

* Founder Name
* Founder Number
* Issue Date
* Official CARDORA Branding
* Founder Appreciation Message
* QR Verification Code

Every Founder Number is unique and assigned only once.

---

# Founder Number

Founder numbers are assigned sequentially.

Example:

Founder #001

Founder #002

Founder #003

...

Founder #500

Numbers cannot be reused once assigned.

---

# Certificate Design

The certificate uses a premium design featuring:

* Black background
* Gold accents
* Official CARDORA logo
* Decorative border
* Premium typography
* Founder crown icon

The visual style reflects the exclusivity of the Founder Program.

---

# Verification

Each certificate includes a unique QR Code.

The QR Code links to the official Founder verification page where users can verify the authenticity of the certificate.

---

# Download

Founder Members may download their certificate as a PDF directly from the application.

The downloaded certificate is intended for personal use.

---

# Ownership

The Founder Certificate is permanently linked to the Founder account.

It is:

* Non-transferable
* Non-refundable (subject to applicable law and store policies)
* Intended for personal recognition

---

# Future Updates

Future versions of the certificate may include:

* Improved design
* Additional security features
* Enhanced verification
* New branding elements

Previously issued certificates remain valid.
